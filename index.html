Research Interest

During my undergraduate years, I have contributed to research projects at St. Olaf College in different fields, including robotics, pure mathematics, computer vision, natural language processing, and system/networking. Overtime, I have developed an interest in building cyber-physical system (mostly AI system) that are reliable, robust, and have adaptive capability. To be more specific, currently, I am interested in Explainable AI, Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Deep RL as well as how these tools would help improving multi-agents system.
